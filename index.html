About Me

I am Jeromel Pushparaj, an aspiring full-stack web developer currently
pursuing my B.E. in Computer Science at Dr. Sivanthi Aditanar College of
Engineering. I embarked on my programming journey a few years ago during
the lockdown while I was studying in 10th grade. The web technology
particularly fascinated me, prompting me to give it a try. Now, I am
actively learning and developing projects based on my acquired knowledge.
I derive joy from the process of creating something, as I am the type
who values the journey over the end result.

In addition to web development, I have a passion for creating YouTube
videos. One of my beloved hobbies is reading books, with a preference
for non-fiction that encourages positive changes in life, as well as
biographies and autobiographies. I also indulge in thoughtful fictional
books for entertainment.

First and foremost, always remember, Jesus loves you.
